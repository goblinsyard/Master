Service time control

Management the start and end time of your service

This module is ideal for Game Store, CyberCoffe and any merchant that charges its service based on time either by hour or fractions of hours, minutes, you can control time of entry and exit.

Create service based on hour or minutes

Service per Hour

You can charge your service on an hourly basis, the system charges the service based on rounding, for example if your service lasted 00:35:03, 1 hour is charged directly.

Service per Minute

If you want more flexibility you can select the option of charging per minutes, this option counts the service based on minutes elapsed in the stopwatch, without rounding.

In the option based on minutes, you can create the variants you need. example: Internet 15 min, 30 min, 45 min, Warnnig, if you want to create the "hour" variant you must use the nomenclature based on "60 min" minute
